Khobrekar's

beach resort

Khobrekar's Beach resort is situated in Devbag village of Malvan Taluka in Sindhudurg District of Konkan. The resort is located close to the beach. Khobrekar's Beach resort is the first resort in Devbag village. The resort was established by Mr. Sujan Khobrekar ten years ago. The tourists were attracting towards Devbag village, because of scenic nature, beauty of coconut trees and beaches. He started the resort and food facilities. The people were impressed by our service. So, they decided to establish it commercially in the year 2007.
